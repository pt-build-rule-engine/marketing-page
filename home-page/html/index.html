Why Rule-Engine?

Many companies, when a minor decision needs to be made, there are often many people involved. When the decision is as minor as getting a new office chair, holding a meeting with your manager, her boss, those finance guys, and that girl that places all the orders can be a waste of time for something that seems so easy. The alternative is write up a form, email it to everybody, make sure it gets approved in the right order, and hope it doesn't get lost somewhere along the way. This can sometimes take weeks! Luckily for you, there is Rule-Engine! This is an app that streamlines and organises this process, and makes sure the right people get the right information so that the right decision can be made as quickly as possible!
